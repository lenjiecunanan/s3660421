Business Chemistry has four pillars of working styles. These are:Pioneer, Integrator, Driver and Guardian. This test helps in developing a hunch about other people’s working styles through observation and also helps in identifying ways to engage more effectively with individuals and a team.

My result from the test outlined that I am mostly 49% Guardian and 40% Integrator .

Guardians are you tend to be a planner, easily frustrated by people and things that waste their time, relationship-oriented, and realistic. They like concrete details and stability. Thye respect what is tried and true.
Guardians are also the most introverted type. They view silence as a productive and important part of any conversation, as it gives them time to think before they speak and plan their words carefully.

Although I am highly aligned with the Guardian pattern, I differ from most Guardians in that I am rated as spontaneously generous, adaptable, and someone who appreciates colleagues with diverse skillsets.

Integrators have two common sub-types. The Teamer is more externally focused and feels a strong sense of duty to society. The Dreamer is more inwardly focused and intrinsically motivated.
I lean more towards as a Teamer.

And again, although I am highly aligned with the Integrator pattern, I differ from most Integrators in that I am easily frustrated by people and things that waste my time, I am also skeptical, and focused.

Understanding individual working styles in a team makes it easier in identifying strengths and assigning roles accordingly to it. It also helps in identofying the weaknesses in a team, makes it easier for them to outline the areas that requires improvement.
